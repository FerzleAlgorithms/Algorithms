AI Resources

A list of AI-related links of interest that I have come across or have been recommended to me. Not strictly related to the algorithms content here, but close enough and this seemed like a good place to put it.

Articles/Books

  • AI Safetey, Ethics, & Society Textbook The site hosts Introduction to AI Safety, Ethics, and Society, a textbook and free online course by Dan Hendrycks (of the Center for AI Safety). It explores how AI's rapid advancement poses societal risks and insists that safety isn't just a tech problem—it demands insights from engineering, economics, and more, covering topics like catastrophic risks, safe design, ethics, governance, and collective action.
  • A Better Way to Think about AI (The Atlantic) This Atlantic piece argues that instead of chasing after full automation—like catapulting AI across a canyon—we should build bridges of collaboration, using AI to enhance work with doctors, teachers, lawyers, and more rather than automate them out of existence.
  • What is ChatGPT Doing and Why Does it Work? (Stepehn Wolfram) Wolfram explains that ChatGPT doesn't "understand" in any mystical sense; it simply predicts the next token in a sequence based on patterns across billions of words, constructing responses word by word according to what's statistically plausible. He also points out that adding a dash of randomness—tweaked via the "temperature" parameter—lets the model avoid bland repetition and produce outputs that feel more creative, even though the underlying process remains entirely probabilistic.

Latest News

  • news.smol.ai news.smol.ai is a daily AI newsletter that condenses the chaos of Reddit, Twitter/X, Discord, and other sources into quick, curated updates for developers and engineers. It’s pitched as a time-saver, giving over 80,000 readers a digestible snapshot of the latest AI models, tools, and debates.
  • LM Arena LM Arena is a platform that allows users to compare and evaluate different language models (LMs) through side-by-side testing. It provides a leaderboard showcasing various LMs based on performance metrics, enabling users to see how models stack up against each other in real-world scenarios.

Platforms

  • Together AI Together AI is a cloud-native platform that handles the entire generative AI lifecycle—from training and fine-tuning to inference and deployment—focusing on open‑source models. It offers fast, scalable infrastructure (think APIs, GPU clusters, bare metal or cloud), built for developers and enterprises that want control, performance, and cost‑efficiency.
