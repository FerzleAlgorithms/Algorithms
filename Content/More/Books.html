Other Books

Here are some of my more traditional open-access textbooks. Unlike this website, these books were written with very little assistance from AI. They were written the old fashioned way—with blood, sweat, and tears. If for some reason you want access to older versions of these, let me know.

  • An Active Introduction to Discrete Mathematics, Version 4.0, Charles Cusack, 2025.
    An updated version of my previous book written for a more general audience with no prerequisites assumed. This version has very little algorithm-related content (which explains the title change). It is suitable for computer science students, secondary mathematics education students (particularly in Michigan), and general education students.
  • An Active Introduction to Discrete Mathematics and Algorithms, Version 3.5, Charles Cusack, 2024.
    The latest version of my discrete mathematics textbook intended specifically for computer science students. This book works best with students who have had programming and data structures courses.
  • An Active Introduction to Discrete Mathematics and Algorithms, Version 3.0, Charles Cusack, 2023.
    A previous version of my discrete mathematics textbook intended specifically for computer science students. This book works best with students who have had programming and data structures courses. I do not think there is any advantage to this over Version 3.5, but here it is just in case.
