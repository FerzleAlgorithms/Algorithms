About This Textbook

Introduction & Purpose

The purpose of this project is to serve as a comprehensive, open-access resource for algorithm-related courses. It is designed to replace traditional textbooks and make algorithms as intuitive and engaging as possible. By combining interactive demos, clear explanations, and human-verified content (In order to speed up the process, we are heavily leveraging AI (specifically ChatGPT) in the creation of the content—especially the demos), this site aims to bring algorithmic concepts to life for students and instructors alike.

Current focus:

  • Create and refine core content suitable for an Introduction to Algorithms course.
  • Develop interactive demos that illustrate each algorithm step by step.
  • Organize material into “textbook modules” that can be assembled to fit different course structures.

Future plans include grouping existing and forthcoming chapters into cohesive “textbooks” tailored to specific courses (e.g., Intro to Algorithms, Advanced Algorithms, Intro to Data Structures). Until then, please explore individual chapters and demos via the sidebar menu.

    • Although we made an incredible amount of progress this summer, creating content for an entire course is a daunting task. I will continue to work during the fall to finish material that will be needed for the last third or so of my course.
